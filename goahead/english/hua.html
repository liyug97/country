     Chinese painting is a traditional Chinese painting with a long history and fine tradition. It embodies the wisdom, character, psychology, and temperament of the Chinese nation. With its distinctive characteristics and style, Chinese painting has its own system in the world. The traditional painting form of the Han nationality is to use a brush to paint water, ink, and color on paper or paper. This kind of painting is called "Chinese painting" or "Chinese painting" for short. Chinese traditional painting(different from "Western painting"). Tools and materials include brush, ink, Chinese painting pigments, rice paper, silk, etc.. The subject matter can be divided into characters, landscapes, flowers and birds, etc.. The technique can be divided into brushstrokes and freehand writing. Its spiritual core is "ink ink."
