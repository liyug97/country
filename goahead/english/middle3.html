70Th anniversary of the establishment of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Navy

       On April 23, 1949, the Navy of the East China Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army was announced in the White Horse Temple in Taizhou, Jiangsu Province, marking the official birth of the People's Navy. Over the past 70 years, under the leadership of the Communist Party of China, the People's Navy has been born in the midst of war and has grown and grown from scratch,The first domestic aircraft carrier and the first ship of the new 10,000-ton destroyer were successively launched. Behind this is the growing strength of the country. For 70 years, the People's Navy has guarded more than 18,000 kilometers of mainland coastline and guarded 3 million square kilometers of blue land. In the past 20 years and more, they have also participated in more than 12,000 rescue and relief operations, rescued more than 60,000 people, and rescued more than 7,600 ships in distress at home and abroad.
