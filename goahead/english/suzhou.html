Second, Wuzheng Garden, a representative of Jiangnan Garden, is one of the four famous gardens in China, a national key cultural relics protection unit, one of the National special tour participants, and a world cultural heritage. To date, only one of these four titles has been held in the country. Third, Lion Forest, one of the four famous gardens in Suzhou, has a history of more than 650 years. In order to commemorate the relationship between Buddhism and Buddhism, the meaning of Leo in the Buddhist scriptures was taken, hence the name "Lion Forest."
