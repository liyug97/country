Name Explanation

  

Alphabet: CR is the abbreviation of China Railway, namely China Railway; "A" and "B" are company identification codes, representing manufacturers, "A" stands for red dragon color, "B" stands for golden phoenix color; "F "Representing the technical type code, indicating a power-distributed locomotive, is different from: "J" stands for power-concentrated electric vehicle group, and "N" stands for power-concentrated internal combustion engine train.

  

Body color

  

The Chinese standard EMUs in the lab, "Red Dragon" and "Golden Phoenix", are red and gold, respectively.

  

Three levels

  

The new naming rules were confirmed by the Iron General Party Group meeting at the end of November 2016. In the future, the Chinese EMUs will be named CR200/300/400, corresponding to three sustained speed grades of 160, 250 and 350 (km/h). Represents the top speed, for example, 400 stands for a maximum speed of 400km/h and above, and a continuous running speed of 350km/h.

  

The three speeds meet different market demands. China's high-speed railways are mainly 350-250 or 250-speed, and the China Express Railway is 200-160. Three speed trains can meet these four speed requirements, and the CR200 is compatible with both fast rail speeds.

  

The "Revival" has two models, "CR400AF" and "CR400BF". China Railway Corporation stated that it will gradually develop CR300 and CR200 series of Chinese standard EMUs according to the needs of the transportation market.
