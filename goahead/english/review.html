Review of "Severe, My Country": Never forget the beginning, my heart is surging.



Source: Qilu Net   Author: Fu Biao   2018-03-04 07:04:03

       Since the Eighteenth National Congress of the Communist Party of China, the Chinese people have created one after another "Chinese miracles" with the good sentiment of fearing hardships, working hard and forging ahead. On March 2, the film "Severe, My Country", produced by CCTV and China Film Company Limited, broke the box office record of the first day of the release of Chinese documentary films, with a film attendance rate of 38.8%. This documentary, which reflects the development and achievements of China since the 18th National Congress of the Communist Party of China and Xi Jinping's socialist thoughts with Chinese characteristics in the new era, has been loved by the audiences all over the country.
       At present, the film scored as high as 8.5 points on the Internet. After watching the film, a large number of audiences have left messages on the Internet platform to praise the film. "Crazy call for our great motherland!" "Praise for the great motherland and the great new era!" "There is a kind of luck to call me Chinese!" "It's so exciting, I feel so proud!" "Severe, My Country" concentrates the rapid development of China in the past five years, records many historical moments of super-ignition, condenses China's strength, carries forward the Chinese spirit, highlights the lofty beauty, strengthens our national prestige, makes the audience see the passion, blood boiling, one after another with "shock" and "pride" and other descriptions.
       China's miracles leap onto the screen. "Severe, My Country" from the dream project, innovation-driven, coordinated development, green China, sharing a well-off society, opening up China and other aspects, shows the historical achievements of China's reform and opening up and socialist modernization since the eighteenth National Congress of the Communist Party of China from a grand perspective. The film not only records the extraordinary super-projects of China Bridge, China Road, China Railway, China Port and China Net, but also shows the milestones leading people to a new era, such as the largest radio telescope FAST in human history, the world's largest offshore drilling platform "Blue Whale 2", the research and development of magnetic levitation train, 5G technology and so on. General scientific research results. When scenes of magnificent aerial photography pass by, the shock and pride brought by China's strength and great power come to us.
       The stories of ordinary people are inspiring. In addition to displaying brilliant achievements, The Great, Our Country organically combines personal dedication at work and social development, presenting the national people's positive, self-confident and enterprising spirit. Many audiences were surprised, surprised and cordial after watching the movie. They deeply felt that "big country" and "small family" are closely related and inseparable. "We are all participants and witnesses of building the Chinese dream together." Only a struggling life is a happy life. Many audiences have a deep feeling that "Severe, My Country" has super positive energy, it calls on each of us not to forget the first heart, with the struggle to write regretless life.
       Where did these brilliant achievements come from? Where does the sense of attainment and happiness of the people come from? The answer is self-evident. It is under the leadership of the Party Central Committee with Comrade Xi Jinping as its core that the people of all nationalities throughout the country have worked hard and earnestly since the Eighteenth National Congress of the CPC. "Severe, My Country" is not only a great power for our country, but also for our Party and our people.
