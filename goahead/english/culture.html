       In 2018, the National Cultural Industry Development Situation analysis
       1. the first half of 2018 the national cultural industry economic operation analysis
       The 40 years of reform and opening-up are the 40 years of vigorous development of Chinese culture and the 40 years of the growing chinese cultural industry. The value added of China's cultural industry as a proportion of GDP has increased year by year, the level of cultural consumption of residents has been continuously increased, the new pattern of cultural going out has gradually formed, and the soft power of national culture has been enhanced.
       In the first half of 2018, 59,000 enterprises in cultural and related industries above the national scale achieved operating income of 422.27 billion yuan, an increase of 9.9% over the same period last year.
       China's cultural industry presents the characteristics of integrated development, digital development, IP development and quality development, and constantly promotes and cultivates new cultural industries. Through the implementation of the "culture plus" strategy, to promote the integration of cultural industries and cultural undertakings, the integration of different cultural industries and the integration of cultural industries and other one- and three-thirds industries; MR technology as the representative of the high-tech in cultural production is more and more extensive, the new digital cultural industry is growing rapidly, the cultural industry pays more and more attention to the role of IP in cultural production, pays more attention to the IP creation of content copyright, some cultural enterprises consciously advocate from "pan-entertainment" to "new creative" quality upgrade, Pay attention to the intension of the development of products to meet the needs of people's upgrading of a better life.

       2. Analysis of the economic operation of nationalization and related industries in 2018
       In 2018, 60 million enterprises in culture and related industries above the national scale achieved operating income of 892.57 billion yuan, an increase of 8.2% over the previous year. By type of industry, the operating income of cultural manufacturing industry was RMB387.4 billion, an increase of 4.0% over the previous year, while the wholesale and retail trade of culture amounted to RMB1672.8 billion, an increase of 4.5%, and the cultural service industry was RMB3445.4 billion, an increase of 15.4%.
       By industry category, 7 of the 9 industries in the cultural and related industries achieved growth in operating income. Among them, the growth rate of more than 10% of the industry has three, namely: news information service operating income of 809.9 billion yuan, an increase of 24.0% over the previous year; There were two sectors with negative growth rates, namely: cultural entertainment and leisure services of 148.9 billion yuan, a decrease of 1.9%, and cultural investment and operation of 41.2 billion yuan, a decrease of 0.2%.
