   Chess, also. Chess player, art also. Game is an important part of Oriental cultural life. It is not only different from ordinary pastime games, but also influences and edifies people's moral concepts, behavioral norms, aesthetic interests and thinking styles. Qin, chess, books and paintings are also called the four traditional art forms in China and have become a cultural form with rich connotations. Between black and white, inside and outside , the enlightenment brought by the chess arts has been infinitely expanded, and the world outside the board has been merged into one, becoming one of the biggest characteristics of Chinese chess culture. Go and chess are the crystallization of the wisdom of the Chinese nation. They are the outstanding traditional cultural heritage of the Chinese nation and contain the time-honored history and heavy cultural deposits of China for 5,000 years.
