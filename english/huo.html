Be honored

Yang Liwei was the first Chinese citizen to enter space (October 15, 2003). Before him, four Chinese astronauts from the United States had entered space.

on November 07, 2003, yang liwei received the title of "space hero" from Jiang Zemin, then chairman of the central military Commission, and won medals and certificates in the great hall of the people.

on February 12, 2004, he was awarded the top ten people who touched China in 2003.

on March 16, 2005, asteroid 21064 was named after yang Liwei.

on July 22, 2008, he was awarded the rank of major general.

on August 06, 2008, the Olympic torch began to pass Beijing's space hero yang liwei to run the first leg.

in November 2008, the fourth China laurel award for aerospace won the laurel award special award.

on September 14, 2009, he was received by all the leading comrades of the standing Committee of the central Committee of the communist party of China as one of the 100 people who have touched China since the founding of new China.

On October 31, 2010, he was selected in the "2010 First Wang Shunli Centennial Chinese People's List".

on September 15, 2014, the 27th annual meeting of the association of space explorers concluded in Beijing. yang Liwei, China's first astronaut to fly into space, was awarded the leonov prize.

on October 27, 2017, yang liwei won the UNESCO medal for space science.

on January 25, 2018, he was awarded the honorary title of "model of the times".

On April 23, 2018, Yang Liwei won the Special Achievement Award of Ceng Xianzi Manned Space Foundation.
