Du Fuguo

  

Du Fuguo, male, Han nationality, November 1991 Born in Taiping Village, Xinglong Town, Meitan County, Guizhou Province, he joined the army in 2010 and was a sergeant of the Yunnan Army Minesweeping Brigade in the Southern Theater.

  

On the afternoon of October 11, 2018, facing unidentified explosives in a complex minefield during a mine-clearing operation at the border, shouting "you fall back, let me come" to the comrades, and suddenly explode when further identifying the situation Heroic injuries, loss of hands and eyes, the same group of comrades are safe and sound.

  

On November 18, 2018, Du Fuguo was awarded a first-class merit. On February 18, 2019, he was awarded the honor of "Touching China's 2018 Person of the Year". On May 22, the Central Propaganda Department awarded Du Fuguo the title of "Time Model". In July, Xi Jinping, the chairman of the Central Military Commission, signed an order to award the honorary title of “Demining Heroes of Heroes” to the Army Du Fuguo.

1Life Resume

  

Du Fuguo, male, born in November 1991, enlisted in December 2010. In June 2015, he participated in the border mine-sweeping operation. In October 2018, he was injured in the protection of his comrades during demining. He lost his eyes and lost his hands. Since joining the army, the comrade has kept in mind the mission, determined to strengthen the army, and volunteered to participate in the border demining mission. He has entered and exited the minefield more than 1,000 times, and has eliminated more than 2,400 explosives and disposed of more than 20 dangerous situations. Master professional skills, explore a series of effective methods, improve the speed and accuracy of detection and blasting; dare to charge in front, use the minefield as a battlefield, endangered the crisis, call "let me come", leave the danger to yourself, Safe to the comrades; to maintain a strong will, even if seriously injured, still high-spirited, strong optimism, adhere to learning and training, his heroic deeds and lofty character in the whole army and the whole society caused strong repercussions.

2People's Deeds


  

On the afternoon of October 11, 2018, the 4th team of the Southern Army Minesweeping Brigade in Yunnan Province carried out mine-sweeping operations at a minefield in Malipo County, Yunnan Province. The operation team leader Du Fuguo brought the soldier Ai Yan in an explosive-intensive mine. During the field search, a small part of the body exposed on the surface was found. The initial judgment was an aggravated grenades with large equivalents and high danger, and a dimple may be buried underneath. Du Fuguo immediately reported to the captain. After receiving the instruction to "find whether there is no trick setting", he ordered Ai Yan: "You step back, let me come!" Ai Yan stepped back a few steps. Just as Du Fuguo carefully removed the floating soil around the projectile in accordance with the operating procedures, a sudden loud bang, the bomb exploded, and he subconsciously fell to the side of Aiyan. The flying shrapnel was accompanied by a strong shock wave, and Du Fuguo’s protective suit was blown into a cotton shape, and he was also blown into a bloody person. Du Fuguo lost his hands and eyes. It was because of Du Fuguo’s moment that he was forgotten to die. Ai Yan, two or three meters away, suffered only skin injuries.

  

After the explosion at 2:40 pm on October 11, 2018, the soldier Du Fuguo was in a coma and shocked on the spot. The injury was very serious. After the on-site military doctors carried out the first aid dressing, the comrades quickly escorted Du Fuguo to the Malipo County People's Hospital for early rescue treatment. At the same time, the mine-sweeping brigade requested further medical assistance from the 59th Hospital of Guilin Joint Service Center. The military hospital quickly transferred a number of experts from the departments of surgery, orthopedics, ophthalmology, burns, skin, etc. to form an emergency rescue team, and organized two ambulances to rush to Malipo County Hospital for assistance and evacuation.

  

After nearly three hours of joint efforts by military and medical personnel, Du Fuguo's postoperative injuries were effectively controlled and vital signs stabilized. Considering that the lung injury may be aggravated from 24 hours to 48 hours after detonation, the county hospital has limited medical conditions. After comprehensive evaluation by the hospital expert group, it was decided to immediately send Du Fuguo to the 59th Hospital of the People's Liberation Army for post-treatment.

  

In the early morning of October 12, 2018, after successfully arriving at the military hospital, a treatment team composed of more than 10 experts conducted an emergency consultation to fully follow up on Du Fuguo's injury. After the emergency treatment in the early stage, Du Fuguo's injury was basically stable. The next treatment was mainly the rehabilitation treatment and psychological intervention of wound repair function.

  

On October 21, 2018, Du Fuguo’s parents rushed to the 59th hospital in Yunnan Province after receiving the notice from the army.

  

On October 28, 2018, the party committees and governments of Malipo County, Maguan County, Guizhou Province, Zunyi City, and Meitan County of Yunnan Province had specially sent a condolence group to visit the hospital for condolences.
