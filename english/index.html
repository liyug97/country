Glorious deeds

manned space flight

At 18 o'clock on September 22, 2017, the Tianzhou-1 cargo spacecraft was controlled to derail. At this point, the second step of China’s manned spaceflight project was completed, and striding into the “space station era”.

Jiaolong exploring the sea

As the only deep-sea manned submersible being used in China, the Xiaolong has an important role in exploring the deep sea. The depth of dive is a symbol of the country's ability to explore the deep sea. Among the world-class scientific manned submersibles, only the Nine Dragons can reach a working depth of 7,000 meters.

Single-caliber radio telescope

As the world's largest single-caliber radio telescope, the completion of FAST has pushed Chinese astronomical research into a deeper world: it has created a new model for the construction of giant telescopes with independent intellectual property rights and is believed to remain in the next 10 to 20 years. World-class status.

super computer

9.3 billion times per second! This is the floating point operation speed of "Shenwei·Taihu Light". In June 2017, in the latest list of the world's top 500 supercomputers released in Germany, “Shenwei·Taihu Light” took the third place in the list with this “super speed” and achieved three consecutive championships.
