Character evaluation

In 2003, Hong Kong's "Ta Kung Pao" published an editorial entitled "Yang Liwei's whirlwind moves the city". The editorial said: Yang Liwei is the symbol and representative of the successful manned space flight of the "Shenzhou" 5 spacecraft. From Yang Liwei's performance, people can see the rise of China's elite generation today, their wisdom and performance, the results of deepening reform and opening up, and the brilliant and far-reaching prospect and hope of the country in the future.

comrade-in-arms commented on yang Liwei: he loves to study and is willing to give.

the media commented on another courage of yang liwei who "did not see the great wall": yang liwei, as the first person in China's space, has the courage to explore and not be afraid of sacrifice; What is even more valuable is another kind of courage-the courage of seeking truth from facts and telling the truth.
