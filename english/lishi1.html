Historical contribution

First, Mao Zedong guided China onto the road of socialist development and established the basic socialist system.

second. Mao Zedong led the Chinese people to open up the road of socialist modernization and began a new era of realizing the great rejuvenation of the Chinese nation along the socialist road.

Third. a new era in which the people are masters of their own affairs has been ushered in and the arduous and tortuous exploration for the realization of socialist democracy has begun.

Fourth, it has established the ruling position of the Communist Party of China and has made unremitting exploration to maintain the advanced nature and ruling position of the Marxist party.

fifth. it has established new China's position as a major country in the world and has made unremitting efforts to create an independent and peaceful diplomacy.
