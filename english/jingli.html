Working experience

Yuan Longping was a former member of the 12th National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, vice-chairman of the Hunan Provincial Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and vice-chairman of the Hunan Provincial Association for Science and Technology. Honorary Dean of School of Agronomy and Biotechnology, Southwest University, Professor of Hunan Agricultural University, Visiting Professor of China Agricultural University, Honorary Dean of Huaihua Vocational and Technical College, Honorary Dean of Hunan Biomechanical Vocational and Technical College, Chief Consultant of FAO, Honorary Chairman of World Chinese Healthy Eating Association, Economic Development Advisor of Yanshou County, Heilongjiang Province.

from January 1971 to June 1984, a researcher at the rice research institute of Hunan academy of agricultural sciences and a member of the provincial hybrid rice research cooperation group.

In 1979, he served as a member of the Science and Technology Committee of the Ministry of Agriculture, vice-chairman of the China Crop Society, director of the China Genetics Society, director of the Hunan Biological Society, and the State Council awarded the title of China's Advanced Science and Technology Worker and China's Model Worker to Yuan Longping, vice-chairman of the Hunan Genetics and Breeding Society, and director of the Hunan Agricultural Society.

on August 26, 1982, he was appointed as the technical consultant of the Ministry of agriculture, animal husbandry and fishery and the deputy head of the China hybrid rice expert advisory group.

On June 15, 1984, Hunan Hybrid Rice Research Center was established with Yuan Longping as its director.

in 1985, he was appointed honorary president of Anjiang agricultural school in Hunan province and part-time professor of southwest agricultural university.

from January 1988 to may 1995, vice chairman of the Hunan provincial Committee of the Chinese people's political consultative conference, director of the Hunan hybrid rice research center, honorary president of the Hunan academy of agricultural sciences.

on November 2, 1991, he was honorary president of Hunan academy of agricultural sciences. In the same year, he was employed as the chief international consultant of FAO.

from may 1995 to January 2016, vice chairman of the Hunan provincial Committee of the Chinese people's political consultative conference and director of the national hybrid rice engineering technology research center (elected academician of the Chinese academy of engineering in 1995).

On July 17, 2003, the Hainan Provincial People's Government held a ceremony in Sanya City, inviting Academician Yuan Longping as the senior scientific and technological advisor of the Hainan Provincial Government.

in 2005, he was hired as a consultant of Guizhou academy of agricultural sciences by Guizhou provincial people's government.

in may 2008, he served as honorary president of Hunan bio-electromechanical vocational and technical college.

In June 2012, Yuan Longping was formally employed as "Senior Advisor of Chen Guangbiao's Green Food Industry".

in January 2013, he was elected vice-chairman of the Hunan provincial Committee of the Chinese people's political consultative conference, and in March he was elected standing member of the 12th China Committee of the Chinese people's political consultative conference.

In January 2016, Yuan Longping resigned from the post of Vice Chairman of the Hunan Provincial Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference.

on Sept. 13, 2018, he was elected as a "scholar" of the Chinese invention association.
