1 retirement age

  

According to the Interim Measures on the Retirement and Retirement of Workers issued by the State Council in June 1978 and the Interim Measures for the Resettlement of Old and Weak Vulnerable Persons (Guo Fa [1978] No. 104), the following situations may be Retirement:

  

(1) Male cadres and workers are 60 years old, female cadres are 55 years old, female workers are 50 years old, and they are working or working for 10 years.

  

(2) Workers engaged in underground, high altitude, high temperature, heavy physical labor and other harmful health workers, male 55 years old, female 45 years old, continuous working or working years of 10 years.

  

(3) Employees who are 50 years of age, 45 years of age, or have a working age of 10 years or more, have been certified by the hospital and confirmed by the labor appraisal committee, and have completely lost their ability to work.

  

(4) Due to work disability, the hospital has proved that the worker (confirmed by the labor appraisal committee) is completely incapacitated. According to the Regulations on Industrial Injury Insurance (implemented on January 1, 2004), employees are identified as disabled from Grade 1 to Grade 4 due to work disability, retaining labor relations, withdrawing from work, and enjoying disability allowance on a monthly basis. After the injured workers reach the retirement age and apply for retirement, they will stop the disability allowance and enjoy the basic pension insurance. If the basic endowment insurance benefits are lower than the disability allowance, the work injury insurance fund will make up the difference.
