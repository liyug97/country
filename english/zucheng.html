Composition structure

Hardware

the hardware composition of a supercomputer is basically the same as that of a personal computer, mainly consisting of an arithmetic unit, a controller, a memory, an input device and an output device. However, supercomputers are incomparable to personal computers in terms of super-strong data analysis and processing capacity, super-large storage capacity and huge energy consumption.

software

high performance computers are very powerful in terms of hardware, but at the same time, in order to give full play to its maximum performance, software is also very important. More than 60% of supercomputers now use Linux operating system, and most supercomputer systems are tailored to adapt to different applications based on Linux.
