Selected Works of Deng Xiaoping

selected works of Deng Xiaoping is an anthology of Deng Xiaoping's works and speeches, edited by the document editing Committee of the CPC central Committee and published by the people's publishing house. By 1993, three volumes had been published.

Deng Xiaoping on education

the first and second editions of Deng Xiaoping's theory of education were edited and published in 1990 and 1995. they include 38 and 53 speeches, talks and inscriptions on education from 1951 to 1989, respectively. The publication of Deng Xiaoping's Theory of Education has played an important role in understanding and mastering Deng Xiaoping's educational thoughts and promoting the reform and development of China's educational cause.

official biography

in December 2009, Deng Xiaoping's chronology (1904-1974) was edited and published by the central literature research office. Based on a large number of documents and archives, the book comprehensively reflects the life experience and ideological course of Deng Xiaoping, a great man of a generation.

on August 19, 2014, with the approval of the central Committee, Deng Xiaoping's collected works (1949-1974) and Deng Xiaoping's biography (1904-1974), edited by the literature research office of the central Committee of the communist party of China, were published by the people's publishing house and the central literature publishing house respectively, and will be distributed nationwide from now on. This is the first official biography of Deng Xiaoping.

the three-volume anthology has 406 manuscripts with more than 800,000 words, most of which are published for the first time. Some of Deng Xiaoping's manuscripts during the "Cultural Revolution" were disclosed to the public for the first time.
