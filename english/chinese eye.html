Chinese Eyes

  

Chinese Eyes refers to the Guizhou Pingtang spherical radio telescope, introduced Its related terms are: 500m caliber spherical radio telescope, FAST telescope, the world's largest telescope, FAST project.

  

The "China Tianyan" scenic spot includes two parts: the FAST Observation Deck and the Pingtang Astronomical Science and Culture Park.

1Development

  

The 500-meter spherical radio telescope (FAST), known as the "Chinese Eye", is the world's largest single-caliber, most sensitive radio telescope. It was completed in September 2016 in Guizhou.

  

In March 2017, Liao Fei, deputy of the National People's Congress and Director of the Science and Technology Department of Guizhou Province, said on the opening day of the Guizhou delegation that the world's largest single-caliber radio telescope was completed in September 2016 in China. It is still in the commissioning phase and is expected to enter the trial operation phase in the second half of the year.

2Travel


  

The "China Tianyan" scenic spot has received 7,000 visitors. The number of tourists entering China's Tianyan scenic spot on the first day was 12,000.

  

Since the 500-meter spherical radio telescope, which was hailed as "China Eye" on September 25, 2016, more and more people want to see the sky. In order to ensure that the “Eye of the Eyes” has a good radio wave signal receiving environment and ensure the safe operation of the “China Tianyan” viewing platform, the scenic spot will implement flow control and online reservation for visitors who come to visit. According to calculations, the number of tourists entering the “China Tianyan” scenic spot during the golden week will reach 10,000 or more per day, while the viewing platform will have a limited number of tourists per day. However, on October 1st alone, the traffic volume reached 800 vehicles/hour, and more than 10,000 tourists. In the past few days, the number of tourists in the scenic spots has greatly exceeded the number of qualified people. The “China Tianyan” scenic spot has already been overloaded. In response to the peak passenger flow, the traffic police department of Pingtang County dispatched more than 40 police officers and 20 vehicles to carry out road traffic. Han Yufeng, captain of the 4th Squadron of the Traffic Police Brigade of Pingtang County Public Security Bureau, said that during the National Day, there will be 10 police forces in each of the three counties and cities in the whole state for a total of 30 people to reinforce.
