Historical evaluation

general comment

although the Qin dynasty lasted only 15 years, the rule pattern established by Qin Shi Huang continued. Qin Shihuang was the main designer of China's centralized feudal monarchy for more than 2,000 years. His influence was deep and wide, and could not be compared with that of any other emperor. In world history, there are still many emperors who created a huge empire, but their actual influence cannot be compared with Qin Shihuang.

China's population has always accounted for more than 20% of the world's population in history. if it has an important influence on China, it can be regarded as having an important influence on the world. moreover, due to centralization of power and loyalty to the monarch, emperors have far greater influence on China's history than on many other countries. From this, we can see Qin Shihuang's vital role to China and even the world.

western evaluation

westerners have always feared China's vast territory. the difference is that Europe is always full of small countries while China is a unified country. This is mainly due to political and social factors, rather than geographical factors such as mountain barriers. Of course, Qin Shihuang played an important role in the unification of China.

in the west, people often compare Qin Shi Huang with Caesar, the Roman emperor. Generally speaking, the population and area difference between the Roman Empire and the Qin Dynasty was not too great. But it was shorter than the Roman empire. After Caesar's death, the empire fell apart. The Qin Dynasty did not. This is the reason why Qin Shihuang had a unique influence. Therefore, Qin Shihuang is almost always ranked the highest or near the highest in any world ranking list of outstanding emperors.
